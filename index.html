Introducing Lobbylayer.

A Chrome extension that provides transparent reporting of Swiss PM's vested interests. On your favourite news site.

Download

Data source: Lobbywatch.

Lobbywatch is a Swiss organisation of journalists and developers that researches information on vested interests of Swiss parliament members and their guests.
